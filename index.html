ABOUT 10-DUKE

10-Duke -- an easily approachable and united class, consisting of 24 students, worked together to successfully execute their juice booth for the Reedley Fair. Though the bid of 18k PHP was surprising, we believed we could achieve it.

With different committees assigned for this project—such as the design and marketing teams—everyone contributed equally. Given the Y2K theme, the design committee had the power to create the logo and select the booth's name.
