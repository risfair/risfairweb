ABOUT 10-DUKE

10-Duke, a class consisting of 24 students had worked together to successfully execute their juice booth for the reedley fair. Though the bid of 18k php was surprising to the class, we all believed we could achieve it. One may describe it as united and close.

With different committees assigned for this project, such as the design and the marketing committee -- the class contributed equally. As the theme of Y2K was given to us, the design committee had the power to make the logo and pick out options for the name.
